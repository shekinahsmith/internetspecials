Wireless Functionality

The U-verse Wireless Gateway connects all of your U-verse services: home phone and high speed Internet. It enables wireless networking, so you can get online anywhere in your home.

Safety & Security

U-verse offers powerful protection online, including virus and spyware protection, SpamGuard to filter out junk mail, Pop-up Catcher to prevent annoying ads online, parental controls and much more!

Convenient Services

Get loads of extras like the AT&T Toolbar which gives you one-click access to Yahoo! pages and services. Plus integrated Flickr access, and enhanced communication with AT&T Yahoo! Messenger!

As a U-verse customer, get on-the-go access to the entire national AT&T Wi-Fi Hot Spot Network, at no extra charge*. That means nationwide hotspots at your favorite stores, hotels, airports, and more.
